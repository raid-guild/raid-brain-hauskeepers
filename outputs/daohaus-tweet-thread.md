## Thread

### 1/7

DAOhaus is community-owned infrastructure for purpose-driven governance.

Keeping that infrastructure maintainable is part of stewarding the ecosystem.

Over the last phase of work, DAOhaus has strengthened the maintenance foundation around the Admin App and related support surfaces.

### 2/7

The goal was simple:

Make DAOhaus easier to understand, support, document, and hand off to future contributors and maintainers.

That means clearer paths for the people who use DAOhaus, build with Moloch, and help keep public-goods governance infrastructure moving.

### 3/7

What changed?

- Clearer Admin App maintenance path
- More durable docs and maintainer guidance
- Cleaner support and repo surfaces
- Better handoff context for future contributors
- A stronger foundation for AI-assisted maintenance workflows

### 4/7

This does not change what DAOhaus is here for.

DAOhaus remains open-source, community-owned coordination infrastructure for DAOs, builders, proposals, safes, members, and public goods.

The work makes the stewardship layer easier to navigate.

### 5/7

AI-assisted maintenance is part of the new foundation, but not as an autonomous replacement for maintainers.

The workflow is designed to help with context, triage, investigation, and implementation support while keeping human review at the center.
