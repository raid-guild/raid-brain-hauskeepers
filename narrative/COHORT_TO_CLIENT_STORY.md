## The Arc

### Act 1 — The Cohort Exploration

<!-- Fill in as you recall / gather details -->

Raid Guild's Dark Factory cohort was exploring agentic development tooling — what tools exist, what works, and what a guild-grade AI-assisted maintenance practice might look like. The cohort was not yet attached to a client. The work was exploratory: evaluating frameworks, prototyping workflows, building intuition.

**Key questions the cohort was investigating:**
- What does an agentic development environment actually look like in practice?
- Which tools are production-ready vs. experimental?
- What's the right level of human-in-the-loop for maintenance workflows?
- How do you structure a codebase so AI agents can navigate it effectively?

### Act 3 — The Raid

The cohort exploration became the foundation of the raid proposal. Rather than starting from zero, RG came in with:
- Evaluated tooling options
- A point of view on agentic maintenance architecture
- Patterns and practices from the cohort work

This gave the raid a head start and gave DAOhaus confidence they were working with a team that had genuine expertise, not just capacity.

- Maintenance SOPs and playbooks
